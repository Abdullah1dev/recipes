This is the recipe of macroni

For the Macaroni: For the Cheese Sauce:
  • 8 oz (225g) macaroni
  • Salt, for the boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (475ml) milk
  • 1 cup (115g) grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup (55g) grated mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
Instructions:

Cook the Macaroni:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the macaroni and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the macaroni and set aside.

Make the Cheese Sauce:

  1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour and whisk together to make a roux, cooking for 1 minute.
  3. Slowly pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until it thickens, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove the sauce from the heat and stir in the cheddar and mozzarella cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

Combine Macaroni and Cheese Sauce:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked macaroni and cheese sauce.
  2. Stir until the macaroni is well coated with the cheese sauce.
  3. Transfer the macaroni and cheese to a baking dish and top with additional grated cheese if desired.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the macaroni and cheese is heated through.
