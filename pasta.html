This is Pasta Recipe

For the Pasta: For the Tomato Sauce:
  • 8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or fusilli)
  • Salt, for the boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups (475ml) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)
Instructions:

Cook the Pasta:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining the spaghetti.

Make the Tomato Sauce:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent (about 3-4 minutes).
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, and dried oregano.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer the sauce over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Combine Pasta and Sauce:

  1. Add the cooked pasta to the tomato sauce.
  2. Toss everything together, adding some reserved pasta water if the sauce seems too thick.
  3. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
